Our Story

We built the game
fans deserved.

We've always been fans first. Fans of creators, of communities, of the way the internet makes stars out of people who just started making stuff in their bedroom. And like a lot of fans, we spent years yelling "this person is going to blow up" — only to have no way to prove it.

The Creator Economy is the biggest thing happening.

Creators now move culture. They shape music charts, buying decisions, trends, and conversations faster than any traditional media ever could. There are hundreds of millions of them — and billions of people following along. But the fans who spotted them early? They got nothing except a screenshot and an "I told you so."

"What if knowing creators before they blow up actually meant something?"

That question is why Virezza exists. We wanted to build a place where your taste, your instincts, and your insider knowledge of who's about to pop off — actually matter.

Competition makes fandom more fun.

Fantasy sports proved it. When your picks have stakes, you watch differently. You root harder. You care more. The same magic works with creators. When you've got a portfolio riding on your favorites — and your friends are competing against you — every viral moment, every new upload, every follower milestone hits different.

Virezza isn't about money. It's about the oldest thing in fandom: bragging rights. And finally, those rights are earned by the ones who were right all along.

Where we're headed.

We're building a world where being a great fan is a skill. Where discovering the next big creator earns you something. Where competition between friends happens in real time, fueled by real culture.

We're just getting started — and we want the people who get it earliest to be part of shaping what this becomes.
